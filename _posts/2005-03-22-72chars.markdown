<p>I like to make cron jobs to mail me interesting information, e.g. the output of
clog. Often, I want to truncate at 72 characters rather than deal with the
wrapping, so I use this cute little sed script:
    sed "s/^\(.\{72\}\).*$/\1/"</p>
